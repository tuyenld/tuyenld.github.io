## Động từ avoir (= to have, tuổi)

Động từ `avoir ` dùng để :
- Chỉ sự sở hữu: tôi có 
- Nói về sự mang mặc: tôi mặc áo đỏ -> J'ai un rouge T-shirt
- Nói về tuổi: tôi 100 tuổi -> J'ai cent ans.
- Diễn đạt cảm giác: tôi đói -> J'ai faim.


| Avoir                  | E.g                      |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------ |
| j'ai [jé]              | I have                   |
| tu as [tu-á]           | You have - informal      |
| vous avez [vu-za-vế ]  | You have - formal/plural |
| il a [in-á]            | He has                   |
| elle a [en-á]          | She has                  |
| on a [on-ná]           | We have - informal       |
| nous avons [nu-za-vón] | We have                  |
| ils ont [in-zón]       | They have    (masculine) |
| elles ont [en-zón]     | They have    (feminine)  |


## à, de and en

[à, de and en - Easy Learning Grammar French](https://grammar.collinsdictionary.com/french-easy-learning/a-de-and-en)

### à
- à + le = au  
- à + la = à la
- à + les = aux
- [in and to] à + names_of_towns_and_cities (à Paris)
- [in and to] au/aux + names_of_countries (aux États-Unis)

| à         | Exemple                                      | Example                                |
| --------- | -------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| # a       | il a (avoir)                                 | he has                                 |
| at        | Nous roulions à 100 km à l’heure.            | We were driving at 100 km an hour      |
| in        | Elle est aux États-Unis.                     | She’s in the United-States.            |
| to        | Je vais au cinéma ce soir.                   | I’m going to the cinema tonight.       |
| to (from) | Je suis en vacances du 21 juin au 5 juillet. | I’m on holiday from 21 June to 5 July. |
| on        | Qu’est-ce qu’il y a à la télé ce soir?       | What’s on TV tonight?                  |
| describe  | la femme au chapeau vert                     | the woman with the green hat           |
| time      | à trois heures                               | at three o’clock                       |
| festival  | à Noël                                       | at Christmas                           |
| distance  | La maison est à 6 kilomètres d’ici.          | The house is 6 kilometres from here.   |
| own       | C’est à toi?                                 | Is this yours?                         |
| hurt      | J’ai mal à la tête.                          | I’ve got a headache.                   |
| goodbye   | À bientôt!                                   | See you soon!                          |

- How something is done
  - fait à la main =	hand-made
- what a type of food is made of
  - un sandwich au jambon =	a ham sandwich
- how you travel
  - On y va à pied?	= Shall we walk?
  - à vélo, à cheval and à pied
  - còn các phương tiện khác là `en, par`
- une tasse à café	= a coffee cup # a cup of coffe
- Je suis prêt à tout =	I’m ready for anything.
- s’intéresser à quelque chose	= to be interested in something

### de

= some or any

Khi kết hợp với mạo từ
- de + le =  du
- de + la = de la
- de + les = des 
- de + "vowel_h_y" = d'

| de         | Exemple                              | Example                              |
| ---------- | ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------ |
| from       | le trajet de Londres à Paris         | the journey from London to Paris     |
| of         | un ami de la famille                 | a friend of the family               |
| # à        | une tasse de café                    | a cup of coffee                      |
| material   | une robe de coton                    | a cotton dress                       |
| used for   | un arrêt de bus                      | a bus stop                           |
| after_most | la plus belle ville du monde         | the most beautiful city in the world |
| qantity    | Combien de personnes as-tu invitées? | How many people have you invited?    |

- Je suis très surpris de te voir.	I’m very surprised to see you.
- If you want to use an adjective after `quelque chose, rien, quelqu’un and personne`, you link the words with de.
  - quelque chose d’intéressant	[something interesting]
  - rien d’amusant	[nothing funny]
- parler de quelque chose	[to talk about something]

## Giới từ 

### entre, en face de, sur, à côté de, près de, de vant, derrière, dans, sous
- à côté de: next to 
- à gauche de: left, on the left
- à droite de: right, on the right
- dans: in, inside
- derrière: behind
- devant: in front of
- en face de: opposite
- entre: between
- loi de: far away
- près de: near by 
- sous: under 
- sur: on (sur la pon, tren cau, sur le toa: tren via he)

#### au vs en
It's almost true to say that "au" is masculine and 
"en" is feminine when they are used with the name of a country. 
(As Erwan says, in other cases, they have essentially separate uses.)

- en is used before feminine countries 
- (and in fact some feminine names of departments, counties, US states...), 
- hence en Espagne, en France, en Italie...
- en is also generally used before any country beginning with a vowel, 
- e.g. en Irak, en Afghanistan

- au is generally used before other masculine countries, 
- e.g. au Canada, au Rouanda, au Mexique

- en = à + le (chỉ đúng với tên nước)

## Phủ định của động từ
- Dùng cặp `ne ... pas`
- Nếu sau `ne` là nguyên âm thì bắt buộc phải viết tắt thành `n'`. 
- Ví dụ: `ne aim -> n'aim`; một ví dụ khác là: `Je ai -> J'ai`


## Nói về sở thích

| Aimer bien | N'aimer pas beaucoup | thích nhiều - không thích nhiều |
